Pricing FAQ

Is there really a free trial?+

Yes — 7 full days with all features. No credit card required. 20 prompts/user/day during trial. Test with your real team before deciding.

Do we pay extra for AI model usage?+

Starter plan includes pool mode — model costs included. Business/Enterprise use BYOK — you enter your own API keys and pay providers directly. Local models (Llama, Mistral) are always free.

What payment methods do you accept?+

Credit/debit card, UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS). Enterprise customers can use POs with net-30 terms. GST invoice provided for all Indian businesses.

What if your company shuts down?+

SecureAI Gateway runs on your own server — your installation continues working regardless. You own your data. Enterprise customers can arrange source code escrow.

Can I upgrade plans later?+

Yes, anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Pricing is prorated for the remaining billing period.
