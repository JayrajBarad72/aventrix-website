The Process

How SecureAI Gateway Works

Every prompt goes through a 6-step security process in milliseconds. Employees see no delay. IT admins see everything.

01

Employee Opens SecureAI

Opens web browser or Windows app. Logs in with company email and password.

02

Types a Prompt

Employee types their question or pastes content. Can also attach PDFs, images, or documents.

03

DLP Scans Instantly

Our engine scans every word for credit cards, Aadhaar, SSNs, emails, phone numbers, and custom rules — in milliseconds.

04

Policy Decision

If clean: prompt proceeds. If sensitive data detected: blocked with clear message. Employee sees exactly why.

05

AI Responds

Clean prompts reach Claude, GPT-4o, or local AI. Response returned to employee instantly.

06

Everything Logged

Full audit trail: who asked what, when, which model, DLP result. Always available to IT admin.

💡 Key point: Steps 1–5 and the logging all happen on your server. Only the clean, DLP-verified prompt reaches the AI provider. Your sensitive data never leaves your network.
