How It Works

6-Step Security Process in Milliseconds

Employees see no delay. IT admins see everything.

01

Employee Opens SecureAI

Opens web browser or Windows app. Logs in with company email and password.

02

Types a Prompt

Employee types their question or pastes content. Can attach PDFs, images, or documents.

03

DLP Scans Instantly

Engine scans every word for credit cards, Aadhaar, SSNs, emails, phone numbers — in milliseconds.

04

Policy Decision

If clean: prompt proceeds. If sensitive data found: blocked with clear message explaining why.

05

AI Responds

Clean prompts reach Claude, GPT-4o, or local AI. Response returned to employee instantly.

06

Everything Logged

Full audit trail: who, what, when, which model, DLP result. Always available to IT admin.

💡

Steps 1–5 and logging all happen on your server. Only the clean, DLP-verified prompt reaches the AI provider. Your sensitive data never leaves your network.
